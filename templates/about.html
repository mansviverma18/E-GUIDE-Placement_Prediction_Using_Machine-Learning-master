PLACEMENT PREDICTION SYSTEM: OUR VISION

The **Placement Prediction System** is engineered to harness the power of data science to guide students through their career journey. Our goal is not merely to predict placement success, but to provide every user with **actionable insights** based on their academic and skill profile to achieve success.

🚀 Methodology: How We Predict

Our system employs a sophisticated **Machine Learning model** to analyze a range of **key input factors**. This model is trained on a large and diverse dataset of historical placement records to ensure the predictions are both accurate and highly reliable.

  • Academic Performance: Evaluates CGPA, 10th and 12th percentages, and the presence of current backlogs.
  • Technical Readiness: Assesses the number of major projects, mini-projects, and specialized certifications completed.
  • Industry Exposure: Measures participation in internships and competitive hackathons.
  • Soft Skills: The Communication Skills rating, which is a critical factor in interview performance.

🎯 Goal Setting and Improvement

The prediction result is presented in a **dashboard** that highlights which factors are **most significantly impacting** your chances (Feature Impact Analysis). If the outcome is 'Not Placed,' the system strategically provides a detailed **Personalized Action Plan** aimed at targeting your weak areas, allowing you to boost your profile effectively.

🔒 Data Security and Privacy

We prioritize the confidentiality and security of user data. All information you submit is used strictly for generating predictions and personalized recommendations. Data utilized to improve the model is processed **anonymously** and in aggregate.
